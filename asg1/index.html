Semester Project

For my semester project, I was thinking about creating a recipe website that helps people make and learn about diabetes and prediabetes-friendly meals. The reason I wanted to make this website is that I know a lot of people who are struggling with diabetes or have impaired glucose tolerance. Therefore, after talking to them, I discovered a lot of people don’t know what they should eat or make due to a lack of access to diabetic friendly recipes. I also learned they sometimes feel discouraged when they can’t go somewhere to find consistent recipes and information. Therefore, I wanted to make a website that is dedicated to making meals that are safe to eat and won’t trigger anyone’s blood sugar. Furthermore, for my website, I plan to have recipes, blogs, images (maybe videos), and ideas about how to make and learn about diabetic friendly meals for diabetics and prediabetics in need.
