Mamamoo Bio

Mamamoo is a South Korean girl band that was created by RBW in 2014 and has been active to this day. There are a total of four girls in the group: Wheein, Moonbyul, Hwasa, and Solar. Mamamoo is known for their strong vocals, expressive writing style in their music, and harmonies as they become a recognizable girl group in the K-pop industry. Since debuting in 2014, the girls have been eliminating stereotypes about gender and fighting against controversial standards in beauty. The girls are presenting themselves in a way that many general K-pop idols will not, challenging criticism and accepted social norms.

Why Mamamoo is My Favorite
